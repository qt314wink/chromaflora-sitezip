About
✦

Hello, I'm Jennipher.

I build interfaces that live and breathe. ChromaFlora is my love letter to the intersection of technology and organic beauty — a design system that treats your screen as a window into a bioluminescent universe.

My work spans interactive design systems, generative art, procedural animation, and creative coding. I believe that the web should be felt, not just seen — every interaction should carry weight, warmth, and a little wonder.

ChromaFlora is built entirely with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no dependencies, no frameworks, no compromises. Every canvas effect, every physics simulation, every chord synthesized is handcrafted code running at native speed in your browser.
