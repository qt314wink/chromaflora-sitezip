## 1. Brand Voice Guide

ChromaFlora's social voice is not a persona — it is a frequency. Every post should feel like it was transmitted from deep ocean or deep space: otherworldly, precise, genuinely curious. Never promotional, never sycophantic.

### Voice Axes
| Axis | ChromaFlora Position |
|------|---------------------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | Precisely casual — expert knowledge in accessible language |
| Earnest ↔ Ironic | Earnest — the work is genuinely strange and we know it |
| Loud ↔ Quiet | Quiet presence — depth, not volume |
| Human ↔ Machine | Human who thinks in systems |

### Voice Don'ts
- Never: "Excited to share..." / "Check this out!" / "Here's a thread 🧵"
- Never: Emoji every sentence
- Never: Generic inspiration quotes
- Never: "Like and subscribe" energy

### Voice Dos
- Specific observations, not general claims
- Questions that don't have obvious answers
- Sharing the *process*, not just the output
- Mathematics as poetry: "E(5,16) is the sound of African djembe written in code"

**Feed (1080×1080 square or 1080×1350 portrait)**
- Mandala exports as the primary visual language
- Color: always on `--void` background
- Caption: 3–5 sentences, never more. Hook in first 125 chars.

### GEO Content Requirements (per page)

Each page should have a document-like description in its HTML that answers "what does this page do?" in 40–60 words. Example for band.html:

```html

It generates Euclidean rhythms using the Bjorklund algorithm, creates chaos patterns via the logistic map, and applies human groove probability. No plugins, no account — free, instant, musical.">

## 9. Visual Storytelling Framework

### The ChromaFlora Visual Language for Social

**Color rule**: All social images use ChromaFlora tokens on `--void` background. Never white backgrounds. Never grey. Non-negotiable.

**Typography rule**: Inter for body. JetBrains Mono for code/data. Both on dark backgrounds with high contrast.

**Image formats by platform:**
| Platform | Format | Safe Zone | ChromaFlora Use |
|---------|--------|----------|----------------|
| Instagram Feed | 1080×1080 or 1080×1350 | Center 800×800 | Mandala exports |
| Instagram Story | 1080×1920 | 14%–80% vertical | Single insight card |
| TikTok | 1080×1920 | 14%–80% vertical | Screen recordings |
| Twitter | 1600×900 | Full | Algorithm visualizations |
| LinkedIn | 1200×628 | Full | Infographic-style |
| YouTube thumb | 1280×720 | Center | Studio screenshots |
| Pinterest | 1000×1500 | Center 900×1400 | High-res mandala exports |

**Visual hierarchy rule**: In ChromaFlora social posts, the order of attention should be:
1. The generative visual (mandala, waveform, grid)
2. A single number or phrase
3. The brand color system as proof
4. Text copy at the bottom
