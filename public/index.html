Design Philosophy

Four Morphism Modes

Style 01
Glassmorphism
Translucent layering with backdrop blur and semi-transparency for depth.
Style 02
Claymorphism
Fluffy 3D shapes with rounded corners, inner shadows and soft inflated look.
Style 03
Neumorphism
Dual light and dark shadows that create extruded or inset soft-UI elements.
Style 04
Material Motion
Unified depth through intentional motion, surface elevation and vibrant color.
